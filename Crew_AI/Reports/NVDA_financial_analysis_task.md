**Company Overview:**
NVIDIA Corporation, headquartered in Santa Clara, California, is a global leader in computing infrastructure, specializing in graphics processing units (GPUs) and artificial intelligence (AI) computing solutions. Founded in 1993, NVIDIA's innovative products are fundamental to advancements in gaming, data centers, professional visualization, and the automotive sector. The company operates through two primary segments: Compute & Networking, which encompasses its data center accelerated computing platforms, AI solutions, networking, automotive, and robotics offerings; and Graphics, which includes its renowned GeForce GPUs for gaming, professional visualization solutions (Quadro/NVIDIA RTX), virtual GPU software, automotive infotainment systems, and the Omniverse platform for industrial AI and digital twins. NVIDIA's comprehensive, full-stack platform, powered by its advanced GPUs and networking solutions, is driving unprecedented performance and efficiency in computing.
