**Company Overview:**
Palantir Technologies Inc. builds and deploys software platforms for the intelligence community and commercial clients. Palantir's solutions are used in counterterrorism, healthcare, finance, and other industries. The company has a strategic alliance with Stagwell Inc. for the development of AI-driven platform for marketers.
